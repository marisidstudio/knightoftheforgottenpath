Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 5, 2026

Knight of the Forgotten Path

Thank you for playing Knight of the Forgotten Path. Marisid Studio respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information our game collects and how it is used.

Information We Collect

Knight of the Forgotten Path does not collect personally identifiable information such as:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Home address

The game can be played without creating an account.

Game Data

The game stores gameplay information locally on your device, including:

  • Save progress
  • Game settings
  • Inventory
  • Character progress

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Analytics

If analytics services are enabled in future versions of the game (such as Unity Analytics or Google Analytics), anonymous gameplay information may be collected to improve gameplay and fix issues.

Examples include:

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • Crash reports
  • Gameplay statistics

No personally identifiable information is intentionally collected.

Advertisements

Current versions of Knight of the Forgotten Path may display advertisements through third-party advertising providers. These providers may collect limited information required to serve and measure advertisements according to their own privacy policies.

In-App Purchases

If future versions include in-app purchases, payment transactions will be securely processed by the platform provider (such as Google Play). We do not receive or store your payment information.

Children's Privacy

Knight of the Forgotten Path is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe personal information has been collected unintentionally, please contact us so appropriate action can be taken.

Third-Party Services

The game may use third-party services including:

  • Unity Services
  • Google Play Games
  • Google Play Billing
  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Crashlytics

Each service operates under its own privacy policy.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any information handled by the game. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
